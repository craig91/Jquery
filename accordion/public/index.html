"We want to introduce the real taste of Japanese ramen to NYC"

The newest addition to Manhattan's Ramen district, Mentoku Ramen stays true to the soul of the Japanese ramen experience. Serving high quality Hakata style "Tonkotsu" ramen at lower prices, Mentoku prides itself in using only the freshest and most flavorful ingredients


About Owner Chef, Yasuo Okada

Trained by a master chef in a classic Japanese "Uotoku" restaurant, Mr.Okada brings his long tradition of Hakata ramen to NYC. After 7n years of studying and successfully running multiple Tokyo resturants, he finally achieved his dream of opening Mentoku Ramen NY in November 2015.
